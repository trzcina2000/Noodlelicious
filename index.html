About Us

When it comes to noodles, one thing that we know for sure is that everyone can eat them. Noodles can be dairy and gluten free, vegetarian, vegan. This helps us overcome restrictions that some of us have when it comes to food. With noodles we can create anything from a deliscious 10 minute dish to a 2 hour dish if we'd like to make our noodles from scratch. Noodlelicious is all about the easy, quick, full of flavour and versatile dishes. You can find two of our favorite noodle recipes in our 'Recipes' page. What are noodles?

Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough which is either rolled flat and cut, stretched, or extruded, into long strips or strings. Noodles are a staple food in many cultures and made into a variety of shapes.
